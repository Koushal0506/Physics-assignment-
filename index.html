Experiment Summary

The Zener diode characteristics experiment is designed to study the voltage-current relationship of a Zener diode in both forward and reverse bias conditions. The experiment helps in understanding the unique property of Zener diodes to maintain a constant voltage across their terminals when operated in the breakdown region.

  • Determination of forward bias characteristics
  • Observation of reverse breakdown voltage
  • Calculation of dynamic resistance
  • Understanding voltage regulation principles
